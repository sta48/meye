THE PROJECT

  1. Distribute menstrual cups to more than 1000 underpriviledged girls and women of Bangladesh.
  2. Hold public workshops to raise awareness about periods to elevate stigma and empower girls.

TIMELINE

NOW - MAY

Finish fund raising and raising awareness about the project.

MAY - JUL.

Contact companies and order menstrual products.

AUGUST

Distribute products and hold workshops about menstruation.

BUDGET

100% of donations will go to the cause.

  • Each cup only costs $2. We plan to use 75% of our funds for the products themselves.
  • The remaining 25% will go into enacting local menstruation awareness workshops.
  • Any remaining funds will be used towards providing more products.
